About Us


Azure Esports is a small gaming organization that is passionate about the competitive scene of gaming. Azure as an organization has been around for years, but only now are we expanding to different games. We have/had divisions in A.V.A, CS:GO, and now have a division in Smite.

We are actively trying out members for our divisions, if you feel you have what it takes to play competitively, either join our TeamSpeak (Our TeamSpeak Info is shown to the right), or send an E-Mail.
